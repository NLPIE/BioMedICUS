NumberContextWriter

open class NumberContextWriter : DocumentTask

Writes numbers and the context of tokens around them to files. The format is one line of up to 6 tokens in the sentence to the left of the number, one line making up all the tokens in the number, and one line of up to 6 tokens in the sentence to the right of the number.
