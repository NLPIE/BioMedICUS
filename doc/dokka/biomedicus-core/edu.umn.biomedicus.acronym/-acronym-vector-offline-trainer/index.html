AcronymVectorOfflineTrainer

open class AcronymVectorOfflineTrainer

Trains an AcronymVectorModel based on text. Does not require the biomedicus tokenizer or other elements of the pipeline. Reads an AcronymExpansionsModel as previously generated by AcronymExpansionsBuilder and finds co-occurrence vectors of all words and phrases corresponding to expansions in text Created by gpfinley on 5/25/16.
